System Assistance

Submitting Timesheets

Help is around 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for submitting timesheets.

If you havent already navigate to the 'Timesheet' tab, from here you will be able to select when you have worked, on which project you have worked on, the date and enter any addtional comments about the work you undertook.

After filling those details in, simpily hit the 'Submit' button, and your timesheet will be submitted.

Contesting Timesheets

If you belive one of your timesheets is incorrect, or you have made an error in one of you submissions navigate to the 'Contest Timesheet' tab and alter specific details about your timesheet.

After that simpily hit 'Submit' to officially contest your timesheet
